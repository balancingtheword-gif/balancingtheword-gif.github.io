# Website-First Publishing Workflow

Balancing the Word Ministry uses the website as the central hub for articles, audio, podcast follow-ups, and archives.

## Publishing Order

1. Publish the full article and audio on the website first.
2. Confirm the website page has the final title, date, image, alt text, audio player or audio link, and summary.
3. Add the page to the Articles + Audio hub and archive.
4. Share previews on Substack, email, and social platforms.
5. Link every preview back to the website page.

## Website Page Needs

Each article-plus-audio page should include:

- Final article title
- Publish date
- Featured image
- Full article text
- Audio player or audio link
- Short summary
- Scripture references, when applicable
- Related podcast follow-up link, when available
- Canonical website URL

## Preview Channels

Use other platforms for excerpts and announcements only:

- Substack: short excerpt and full website link
- Social media: quote, image, and full website link
- Email: brief intro and full website link
- Podcast platforms: episode note and full website link

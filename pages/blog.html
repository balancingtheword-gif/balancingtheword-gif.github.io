The God of Hope: True Hope in Christ

Posted on April 2026

Where is your hope truly placed?

In a world filled with uncertainty, many place their hope in things that cannot last. But Scripture points us to a hope that is secure and found only in Christ.

True hope is not found in the world…
it is anchored in Christ alone.

This message explores the difference between the hope of the world and the true hope found in Christ—and how that hope sustains us.

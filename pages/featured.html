Sanctification: A Call to Be Set Apart

April 2026

What does it truly mean to be set apart for God?

After the Easter celebration, the question remains—how are we living in response?

Are we truly set apart…
or have we blurred the line between what is holy and common?

This message explores the foundation of sanctification—from Israel to the believer today—and challenges where compromise may have taken root.

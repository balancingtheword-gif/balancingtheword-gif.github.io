Privacy Policy

Balancing the Word Ministry

Effective Date: January 31, 2026

Balancing the Word Ministry (“BTWM,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal information you choose to share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or interact with our ministry online.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you voluntarily subscribe to updates, contact us, or communicate with us, we may collect your name and email address.

Usage Data

We may collect non-personal information such as browser type, pages visited, and general usage statistics through analytics tools to better understand how our website is used.

How We Use Your Information

  • Communicate with you regarding ministry updates, studies, or resources
  • Respond to inquiries or messages you send us
  • Improve website functionality and content
  • Maintain the security and integrity of our website

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Analytics & Third-Party Services

Our website may use analytics services (such as Cloudflare or similar tools) to monitor traffic and improve performance. These services collect data in an aggregated, non-identifying manner.

Embedded content or links to third-party platforms (such as Facebook or Substack) may have their own privacy policies. Balancing the Word Ministry is not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Choices

  • Opt out of email communications at any time
  • Request correction or removal of your information

Children’s Privacy

Balancing the Word Ministry does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated effective date.

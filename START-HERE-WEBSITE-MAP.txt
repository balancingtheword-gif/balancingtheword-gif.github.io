This folder is the live Balancing the Word Ministry website.

Most updates happen in these places:

1. Homepage
   - index.html

2. Current featured article
   - articles/featured-article.html

3. Older full articles
   - archive/

4. Article archive listing page
   - pages/blog-archive.html

5. Audio and images
   - Assets/audio/
   - Assets/images/

6. Notes, templates, and reference material
   - _workspace-notes/

Important: Do not delete CNAME, index.html, Assets, pages, articles, or archive. Those are part of the website.

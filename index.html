About Balancing the Word Ministry

Balancing the Word Ministry exists to teach, train, and equip believers with a biblically balanced worldview. Through scriptural instruction and the imparting of godly wisdom, we help believers grow to maturity and become “complete in Christ.” We mentor new believers in faith, spiritual gifts, and discernment, while encouraging mature Christians to teach, mentor, and serve others. In all we do, we proclaim God’s love and His grace given through Jesus Christ.

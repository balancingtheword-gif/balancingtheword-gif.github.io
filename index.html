The Mission

Balancing the Word Ministry exists to teach and equip believers with a biblically balanced worldview. Through Scripture and mentorship, we help believers grow to maturity and become complete in Christ (Colossians 1:28).

Lesli D. Mize

I’m Lesli D. Mize, founder of Balancing the Word Ministry. My desire is to encourage believers with sound, biblically balanced teaching and to point every reader to the love and grace of God through Jesus Christ.

The Mission

Balancing the Word Ministry exists to teach and equip believers with a biblically balanced worldview, helping them grow to maturity and become complete in Christ (Colossians 1:28).

Lesli D. Mize

I’m Lesli D. Mize, founder of Balancing the Word Ministry. My desire is to serve believers by helping them grow in discernment and maturity through biblically balanced teaching grounded in Scripture.

Serving and teaching are at the heart of this ministry. After surrendering my life to Jesus Christ, writing became a way to serve others through studies, blogs, and books—encouraging believers to remain rooted in truth and to grow toward becoming complete in Christ (Colossians 1:28).

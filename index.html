The Mission

Our mission is to build others through Scripture by educating with sound biblical truth and discipling them toward spiritual maturity. (Colossians 1:28).

Lesli D. Mize

I founded this ministry after recognizing the need for others to learn how to read and study the Scriptures for themselves. From that desire, my focus is to help them grow in discernment and spiritual maturity through biblically balanced teaching grounded in Scripture.

Serving and teaching are at the heart of this ministry. After surrendering my life to Jesus Christ, service became a calling expressed in many ways—wherever there is a need—with the aim of helping others remain rooted in truth and grow toward becoming "complete in Christ."

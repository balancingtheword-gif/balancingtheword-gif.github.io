My Story

From an early age, my life was marked by instability, trauma, and loss. Throughout childhood and adolescence, I experienced abuse, frequent displacement, and foster care, learning to adapt quickly while longing for safety, love, and belonging.

Even in those years, God was drawing me to Himself. The Bible and prayer became my refuge—places of comfort, peace, and hope when nothing else felt secure. Though I believed in God and Jesus, I did not yet understand salvation, and for many years I searched for freedom and healing.

In a season of deep brokenness as a young wife and mother, I cried out to Jesus with a surrendered heart. In that moment, I experienced true forgiveness, renewal, and redemption. Shortly afterward, I received the baptism of the Holy Spirit, and my life was forever changed. Since then, God has continually renewed my mind, healed my heart, and transformed my life—giving me a deep desire to serve others, teach His Word, and point all people to the hope, freedom, and power found in Jesus Christ.

My journey stands as a testimony that God redeems, restores, and empowers all who come to Him in faith.

The BTWM Story

Balancing the Word Ministry exists to teach, train, and equip believers with a biblically balanced worldview—helping them grow to maturity and become “complete in Christ”.

Serving and teaching are at the heart of this ministry. Through studies, blogs, and books, BTWM is devoted to strengthening believers in discernment, Scripture, and Spirit-led growth.
